    Built an open source research engine that does what Perplexity and ChatGPT Deep Research charge $20/mo for — except it's self-hosted, BYOK, and actually verifies its claims.
    
      What it does:
      - Deep Research mode: 5,000-10,000+ word reports with 20+ verified sources (~$0.08/query)
      - Academic mode: 200k+ character outputs with Toulmin argumentation and evidence grading
      - ASK mode: Verified quick answers — every claim audited against a second round of sources (~400 answers for $1)
    
    Why it exists:
    I benchmarked ChatGPT, Perplexity and Gemini on the same scientific query. ChatGPT fabricated citations. Gemini minimized data. Perplexity gave surface-level results. Lutum delivered 103k characters with 90
    real sources for $0.19.
    
      Tech:
      - Camoufox scraper (hardened Firefox fork, 0% bot detection)
      - Recursive pipeline with context accumulation between research points
      - Multi-provider: OpenRouter, OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, HuggingFace
      - FastAPI + Tauri desktop app, runs fully local
