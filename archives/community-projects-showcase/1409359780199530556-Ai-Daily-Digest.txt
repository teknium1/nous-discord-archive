    The rapid development of AI brings a constant stream of innovations and hot news. So, I fetched the top AI posts from Reddit. Using an LLM, I summarized the key points and categorized the posts. Through a Telegram bot, I curated the 5 hottest posts and published a daily digest. Additionally, I share the latest news from time to time. This way, you no longer need to manually search for updates.
    
    At the same time, you can chat with this bot just like other AI chat functions. However, this bot integrates all AI-related posts, making the information in the AI field more timely, accurate, and effective.
