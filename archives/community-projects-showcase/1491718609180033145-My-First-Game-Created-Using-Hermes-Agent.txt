    Just deployed my first game with Hermes agent and honestly it was pure fun to build
    
    The idea is simple but addictive. Your main goal is to defeat Boss Rocky to move to the next level. Every enemy you beat drops crystals, and those crystals become your power. Press F to use them in fights and survive the chaos
    
    You can choose your character at the start:
    
    Press 1 to play with character 1
    Press 2 to play with character 2
    
    The game has 10 levels and each level brings a new style and new challenges. It keeps changing so it never feels repetitive. And here’s a small quality of life boost: once you reach level 4, you don’t restart from the beginning anymore
    
    Now the fun part 
    
    If you finish the final level, you’ll get a secret code. Send me the code and the first person who does it wins $10
    
    You can play it here: https://billyuzi-eng.github.io/Seismic-Game/
    
    Give it a try and tell me what you think. I’d love to hear your feedback and see who beats the game first.
