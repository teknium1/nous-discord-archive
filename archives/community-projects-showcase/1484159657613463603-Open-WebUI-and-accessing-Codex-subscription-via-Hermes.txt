    Hi all, I thought I would let you know that if you'd like to interact with LLM models via your codex subscription in Open WebUI, it's actually really easy. I got Hermes to make a new completion endpoint which acts as a proxy between Open WebUI and Codex directly. That way I can point Open WebUI to it and access all the available models.
    
    That means there are two endpoints:
    - Hermes agent endpoint to interact with your agent and all the skills, plugins, etc. that is has
    - Hermes completion endpoint to directly interact with codex models
