    I’m an independent researcher from Brazil working on **Lunaris**, an open-source sparse architecture called **MoC (Mixture-of-Collaboration)**.
    
    The core idea is simple: instead of experts operating purely in isolation, MoC allows them to exchange information through a mediator mechanism.
    
    Recent progress:
    
    validated at small scale against dense and MoE baselines
    refreshed implementation with training and stability fixes
    initial scaling run at **~197M parameters**
    **45.7 validation perplexity**
    **0 dead experts**
    **near-zero routing drop**
    
    Everything is public:
    
    * GitHub: https://github.com/Auren-Research/lunaris
    * Paper: https://github.com/Auren-Research/lunaris/blob/main/paper/lunaris-moc.pdf
    * W&B: https://wandb.ai/smeryylle-moon-cloud-services-/lunaris-moc-validation
    
    I’d love feedback from people interested in sparse models, MoE variants, routing, or open-source AI research.
