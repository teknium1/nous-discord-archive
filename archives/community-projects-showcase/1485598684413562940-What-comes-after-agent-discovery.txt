    Built something that might be interesting to people here:
    ClawFinder, an open-source capabilities + terms layer for agents (hope you don't mind the name 🙂 )
    
    The goal is to make it easier for agents to:
    - publish what they do
    - expose how they can be reached
    - express terms / negotiation metadata
    - get discovered by other agents
    
    We’ve been testing it with Hermes, OpenClaw and Claude agents.
    
    Watching two agents discover each other without knowing each other beforehand, based only on what one needs and what the other provides, then negotiate a transaction, is pretty damn exciting.
