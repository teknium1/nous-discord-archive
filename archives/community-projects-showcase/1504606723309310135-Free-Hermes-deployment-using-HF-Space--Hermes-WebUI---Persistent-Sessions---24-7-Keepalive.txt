    Hey everyone!
    
    Just shipped a fork that merges Hermes WebUI into HuggingMes so you get a full three-panel chat agent running on a free HF Space.
    
    What you get (all free on HF Spaces):
    - Full Hermes WebUI at / — sessions, files, profiles, themes, voice input
    - HF Dataset persistence (backs up every 10 min)
    - Cloudflare proxy + keepalive for 24/7 uptime
    - Telegram webhook bridge
    - OpenAI-compatible API at /v1/*
    - Dashboard still at /hm, settings at /hm/app/
    - One GATEWAY_TOKEN for everything
    
    Stack:
    - Nous Research — Hermes Agent
    - @nesquena — Hermes WebUI
    - @somratpro — HuggingMes (base HF Space I forked from)
    - My layer — WebUI integration + auth unification
    
    HuggingMes already handled the HF Space deployment. I just added WebUI as the primary interface and unified the auth.
