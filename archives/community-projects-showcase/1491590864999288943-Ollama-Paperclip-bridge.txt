    I created a work around for 
    @ollama
     cloud models to work in paperclip thru Hermes Agent.
    
    I'd love some feedback on my implementation until Ollama Cloud is officially supported by both.
    
    The hermes-paperclip-adapter has a hardcoded provider whitelist that silently drops custom providers. Your agents think they're hitting Ollama Cloud but they're falling through to OpenRouter → 404.
    
    The fix: a local Ollama proxy bridge. Three files per agent, zero patches.
    
    📄 Full PDF + 🎬 terminal demo:
    https://github.com/Vivere-Vitalis-LLC/Ollama-Cloud-Hermes-Agent-Paperclip
