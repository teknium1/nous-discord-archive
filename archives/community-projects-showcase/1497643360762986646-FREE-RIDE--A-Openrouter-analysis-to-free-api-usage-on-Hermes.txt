    Hermes Free-Ride
    Robust module for Hermes to use free OpenRouter templates in sub-agents and lightweight tasks, without changing the main primary template.
    
    The idea comes from the original OpenClaw free-ride, but with a focus on the Hermes ecosystem:
    
    Free template discovery
    Filters for truly useful templates for text agents
    Real probes in chat/completions
    Caching with TTL
    Cooldown for problematic templates
    Health check and automatic delegation template rotation
    Secure integration with ~/.hermes/config.yaml
