    A simple knowledge base that lives on your machine and makes collaboration with agents easier.
    
    In one sentence
    Store the machine-readable index in SQLite, keep human-readable state in plain markdown, and make a CLI the only stable integration surface.
    
    Please let me know your thoughts and suggestions, hope this is a useful tool. Thanks!
    
    PS: this was collaborative work with my Hermes agent. When asked for a comment this was the answer: "From the assistant side, the real win is not just search — it’s reliability. A simple local index plus a stable CLI makes retrieval, citations, and handoffs much more dependable than ad hoc memory."
