    I have bunch of local models I have configured and am running locally on my two machines - Mac Mini and Ubuntu.
    
    The whole setup consists of set of generalized scripts that can use llama.cpp or vllm to host a model, model card with context, parallel requests, quant, etc, and launchd / systemd config for each model. I can run a model manually or I can use script to install it as a service.
    
    I also have a small sys tray mac OS app (plus a service on each machine) that I can use to control all my models installed as a service.
    
    Here are two screenshots - the list of services (both models and others) installable and installed on my Ubuntu, and the control center app showing the installed services and models on the same Ubuntu machine.
    
    i think this can be easily cleaned up and open sources, if people are interested in the wholse setup.
