    Hey everyone 👋
    
    I'm Kyzen, part of the core team building LuminOS, a Debian 13–based Linux distribution.
    
    While our baseline goal is to make Linux highly accessible and frictionless for users coming from Windows, our main focus is prioritizing fair and ethical tech, ans treating local, private AI as a core system component. We are integrating local LLMs (using tools like Ollama) directly into the OS to enable offline, privacy-first system assistance & agentic actions without relying on web wrappers or cloud APIs.
    
    Current progress:
    • ISO build scripts are functional but really perfectible (Currently porting from Bash to a concurrent Go/Bubble Tea TUI)
    
    • Early-stage AI packaging with ollama 
    
    • Basic system customization and workflow improvements
    
    The project is still in its early stages, and we are looking for folks who want to get in on the ground floor. We specifically need help with:
    
    • Go development (for the ISO builder rewrite)
    
    • Linux system development (Debian/Calamares installer)
    
    • Testing and breaking our ISOs
    
    • UI/UX improvements (KDE Rice)
    
    • And anything else that makes an OS grow
    
    If you’re interested in contributing, experiencing with local AI, or just want to share ideas, we’d love to have you.
    GitHub: https://github.com/4LuminOS 
    
    Thanks 🙂
