    Agents can’t reliably self-critique. hermes-council fixes this by requiring an external jury debate before an agent commits to an action.
    
    Key Features
    
    - The Jury: Five personas (Advocate, Skeptic, Oracle, Contrarian, Arbiter) debate the task.
    
    -The Verdict: The Arbiter uses Bayesian synthesis to issue a structured allow, allow_with_conditions, or deny.
    
    - Hardened Grounding: Includes 8 specialized tools and SSRF-hardened evidence fetching via URL/DDG.
    
    - DPO Flywheel: Emits preference pairs from internal disagreements to provide a direct training signal for future finetuning.
    
    Quick Start
    
    pip install "hermes-council>=0.1.1"
    
    Uncomment the council block in cli-config.yaml.
