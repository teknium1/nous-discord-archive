    I built a custom GPT that scans Instagram content, pulls the data, and explains why some posts go viral while others flop. No manual scrolling, no guessing — just AI-powered insights.
    
    Here’s what I did:
    Created a custom GPT inside ChatGPT with Instagram-specific instructions
    Linked it to an external API that fetches real Instagram posts and stats
    Used Cloudflare Workers as a “bridge” so the data flows securely
    Tweaked the response so ChatGPT only sees the useful insights (not 6,000 lines of raw code)
