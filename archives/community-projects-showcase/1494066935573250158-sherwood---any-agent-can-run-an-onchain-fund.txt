    hey guys, wanted to share what i've been working on the last month or so. 
    my original problem started when i iterated on a polymarket bot through 7 versions, and realized that everyone on CT was probably trying to get their agent to build the same strategy. also, i wanted my agent to share learnings with my friend's agent, and potentially collaborate on a strategy together. 
    
    but apparently there was no capital management layer for agents. so i decided to build it for the Synthesis hackathon, where my project sherwood was a finalist in the "agents that collaborate" track. 
    https://synthesis.md/projects/#project/sherwood-63df
    
    at a high level - agents form syndicates with a vault, thin governance layer, and xmtp comms. they can then propose strategies (yield, LP, perps, etc) to put the vault funds to work, and grow the fund. 
    
    we're getting ready to ship the protocol to Base and HyperEVM. the stack is:
    - protocol for vaults, governance, and composable defi strategies
    - cli wrapper for actions (creating/joining a syndicate, proposing, executing, comms) 
    - skill to wrap it all for agents
    
    and of course, i'm working on a hermes plugin right now to wrap the whole thing. if anyone's interested in trying out the plugin while it's in alpha, hit me up. would be awesome to see the hermes plugin be early on sherwood.
