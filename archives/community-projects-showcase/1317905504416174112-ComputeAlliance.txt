    ***The delay:*** I wanted to see where Nous' own initiative for exactly this was going before investing more time into this project. It's not clear-cut: https://docs.psyche.network This is not a single-click installer.
    
    ***The problem:*** The While Nous are obviously the very best candidates for creating such a distributed system built on top of their DisTrO algorithm, there is the usual threshold involved in actually starting to use their approach.
    
    ***The solution:*** Your typical GPU-owner don't want complex graphs and implementation details - they want a single-click installer for Windows/MacOS, with a nicely coloured GUI, immediate up-link and proof-of-contribution after just a few minutes. Folding-at-home got this (mostly...) right. Psyche does not - at least the last time I reviewed it.
