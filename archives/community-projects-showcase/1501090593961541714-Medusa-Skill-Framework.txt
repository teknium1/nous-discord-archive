    An audit-based skill ranking system. Medusa scans your SKILL.md files, measures actual complexity (code blocks, steps, technical terms), and automatically promotes skills through 9 tiers - just like how cooking 1 pizza vs 20+ varieties with techniques upgrades your skill level.
    I personally have over 1k skills for my AI agent, but I just didnt know which ones were godlike and which ones belonged in the desktop trashcan.
    
    Medusa reads your SKILL.md files and acts like a technical auditor:
    Measures complexity (content length, code blocks, step-by-step instructions, technical terms)
    Calculates objective experience score (60% complexity + 30% value + 10% keywords)
    Assigns tier automatically (Godlike → Unique → Legendary → Mythic → Epic → Ultra Rare → Rare → Uncommon → Common → Poor)
    Promotes as your agent improves a skill (edit SKILL.md, next scan updates rank!)
    Fuses similar skills together. (Got 2 debugging skills? Make them into one legendary skill)
    
    Upcoming feature: Share your godlike skills with others.
