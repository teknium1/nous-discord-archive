[2026-04-15T23:35:57.469000+00:00] labhund (id=1494119119455391925)
    Purpose
    
    lacuna-wiki is a compounding research knowledge substrate — a personal wiki that accumulates structured claims from academic papers, YouTube videos and Podcasts. grounds LLM agents in your specific compiled knowledge, and resists regression toward consensus synthesis.
    
    graphify is a codebase/collection explorer — it transforms a folder of files into a queryable, interactive knowledge graph for one-shot exploration and discovery, producing graph.html, graph.json, and GRAPH_REPORT.md.
    [reply to chfarhan_ msg=1493895865629282456]

[2026-04-15T23:44:23.418000+00:00] labhund (id=1494121241559306431)
    Where They Overlap
    
    Both address the "too much content, not enough structure" problem. Both use embeddings, both track relationships between concepts. If you were building a tool to explore an unfamiliar codebase quickly, graphify is the right shape. If you're building a research instrument that should get smarter every time you read a paper, lacuna is the right shape.
    The adversary skill (claims verification, supersession, fidelity checking) and the synthesis tool (stitching together ideas to build up a comprehensive understadning and review of a topic) is unique to lacuna and has no equivalent in graphify. That's the core of what makes lacuna a research tool rather than an exploration tool.
