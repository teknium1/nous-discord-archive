    hey! just wanted to share a Spotify skill for Hermes — **hermes-spotify-skill**
    
    works fine on headless Linux / Raspberry Pi (using it on Pi 4 with RPi OS Lite 64-bit).
    
    installation: Just send github repo link to your hermes agent and ask to set it up, they will ask you to help.
    
    what it does:
    - search & play tracks, albums, artists, playlists
    - pause, resume, skip, volume, transfer between devices
    - works with raspotify so your Pi becomes a Spotify speaker Hermes can control
    - no daemon, no server — just spotipy snippets via execute_code
    
    https://github.com/Alexeyisme/hermes-spotify-skill
    
    would love any feedback if you try it out 🙏
